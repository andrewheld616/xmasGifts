Earl Grey Shortbread

Earl Grey
Shortbread Cookies

  • Description: A delicate and fragrant treat that combines the buttery richness of traditional shortbread with the distinctive flavor of Earl Grey tea. Enhanced with vanilla bean and Earl Grey tea leaves, these biscuits have a subtle citrus and floral aroma. The crumbly, melt-in-your-mouth texture makes them perfect for pairing with a cup of tea or coffee.
  • Ingredients: Butter, Powdered Sugar, Flour, Vanilla Bean, Earl Grey Tea, Turbinado Sugar

Empire/Linzer Biscuits

Empire/Linzer Biscuits

  • Description: These shortbread sandwich cookies were originally known as Linzer Biscuits in the UK. After World War One broke out against Germany and their allies, the cookies were re-branded as Empire Biscuits. They consist of two sweet, buttery shortbread cookies sandwiched together with a layer of fruit jam, and are topped with a sweet icing glaze and sometimes garnished with a decorative cherry on top.
  • Ingredients: Butter, White Sugar, Egg, Vanilla Extract, Flour, Baking Powder, Raspberry Jam, Powdered Sugar, Almond Extract, water, glace cherries.

Ginger Molasses Cookies

Ginger Molasses Cookies

  • Description: Soft and chewy treat with rich, warm flavors and a slightly crisp exterior. Features a blend of spices, including ginger, cinnamon, and cloves, which complement the deep sweetness of molasses. Dark brown in color with a rustic appearance, and is rolled in sugar before baking to give it a sparkling finish.
  • Ingredients: Sugar, Butter, Molasses, Egg, All Purpose Flour, Baking Soda, Cinnamon, Salt, Ginger, Cloves, Nutmeg, White Sugar for dusting

Glazed Sugar Cookies

Glazed Sugar Cookies

  • Description: Buttery, sweet flavour with a soft and tender texture. The royal icing provides a glossy finish that hardens to create a smooth surface; these cookies showcase vibrant colors and intricate designs, bringing festive shapes like trees, stars, and snowflakes to life. Adorned with sprinkles, edible glitter, or other festive embellishments, these cookies are perfect for holiday celebrations.
  • Ingredients: Butter, Sugar, Eggs, Vanilla Extract, All-Purpose Flour, Baking Powder, Salt, Powdered Sugar, Water, Food Colouring with Coloured Sugar and Sprinkles for decoration

Chocolate Buttertarts

Chocolate Buttertarts

  • Description: A delicious twist on the classic Canadian dessert, replacing the traditional raisins with rich, creamy chocolate. Nestled in a flaky, buttery crust, these tarts are baked until the filling is set but still slightly soft, resulting in a delightful contrast of textures.
  • Ingredients: Tart shells, Butter, Brown Sugar, Corn Syrup, Egg, White Vinegar, Vanilla Extract, Salt, Melting Chocolate for decoration
